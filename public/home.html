How Does it work?

Glad you asked. Sign in with Twitter. In the message field, write and tag your message with an emotion.

Currently the only emotions available are:

  • Happy
  • Sad
  • Love
  • Hate
  • Party
  • Fail
  • Bored
  • Eyeroll

To tag an emotion, add % before it.

Your tweet should look something like this:

I %love to tweet.
